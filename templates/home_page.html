ABOUT
CIT 8.0

CIT 8.0 is a one-of-a-kind opportunity for all aspiring students as we unveil exhilarating challenges of national standard over the realms of basic mathematical aptitude, logical and verbal reasoning, and data interpretation. Not only does CIT instill an idea of various acclaimed aptitude tests like CAT, but our flagship event also never fails to provide a simulation of the overall screening procedure of hiring companies. This is the most anticipated online event to compete for intellectual glory among a multitude of striving contestants
